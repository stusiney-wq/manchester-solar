Introduction

Over 2,800 homes across Greater Manchester already have solar installed, and adoption is growing by 35% each year.

For homeowners, the average saving is £1,890 per year. For businesses, it's more than £4,200 annually, with typical payback periods of 6–8 years.

This guide explains costs, savings, incentives, property suitability, and how to choose a trusted local installer.

Manchester Solar FAQs

Do solar panels work in Manchester's climate?

Yes. With 3.8 sun hours/day, panels perform well, even on cloudy days.

How much do solar panels cost in Manchester?

Residential systems: £4,000–£8,000, with payback in 6–8 years.

Do I need planning permission?

Not usually, unless your property is listed or in a conservation area.

How long until panels pay for themselves?

Most homes reach payback in under 7 years, with 25+ years of lifespan.

What if I move house?

Solar boosts property value; new owners benefit from lower bills.
