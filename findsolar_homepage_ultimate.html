Trust & Transparency

Our commitment to editorial independence and ethical practices.

Editorial Independence

We are not owned by installers or manufacturers. Our recommendations are based solely on installer quality, customer satisfaction, and local expertise.

  • No manufacturer partnerships influencing recommendations
  • No installer ownership or financial stakes
  • Transparent vetting criteria published publicly
  • Customer feedback monitored continuously

Data & Privacy Promise

Your information is never resold or shared. When you request a quote, your details go directly to one installer only.

  • GDPR compliant data handling
  • No lead auctions or bidding systems
  • No third-party data sales
  • Unsubscribe honored immediately

Revenue Model: We charge installers a monthly fee for exclusive city partnerships and maintain editorial independence through this subscription model rather than per-lead commissions.
