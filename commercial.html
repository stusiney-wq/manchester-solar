What Is MEES and How Does Solar Help?

MEES (Minimum Energy Efficiency Standards) require commercial properties to meet a minimum EPC rating. If your EPC rating is below E, you could face fines up to £150,000—and future targets rise to B by 2030.

⚖️ Legal Requirements

  • Current: EPC rating E or above required
  • 2027: EPC rating C target
  • 2030: EPC rating B target
  • Penalties: Up to £150,000 fines for non-compliance

✅ How Solar Helps

  • EPC upgrades via solar panels (1-2 band improvement)
  • Full documentation for MEES and ESG reporting
  • Site assessments to identify retrofit opportunities
  • Future-proof for 2027/2030 targets
