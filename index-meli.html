Frequently asked questions

Do solar panels work in Manchester’s climate?

Yes. Manchester averages ~3.8 peak sun hours per day. Modern panels perform well in diffuse light, so systems generate even on cloudy days. A professional design matched to your roof and usage maximises yield.

Are period terraces suitable for solar?

Usually yes. Period terraces and semis can host discreet, high‑efficiency arrays. Your installer will check roof structure, shading and access, and advise on placement that suits the street scene.

How do you prevent spam or multiple calls?

We connect you with a single vetted local MCS installer. We don’t auction or resell details, so you won’t receive multiple sales calls. One professional contact, clear pricing and a 24‑hour response.

Do I need planning permission?

Most homes fall under Permitted Development. Listed buildings, conservation areas or unusual installations may need permission. Your installer will confirm and handle any application if required.

How much do solar panels cost in Manchester?

Typical residential systems are ~£4,000–£8,000 depending on roof, usage and equipment. With 0% VAT until March 2027 and average savings near £1,890/year, many households see 6–8 year payback.
