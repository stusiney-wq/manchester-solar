Norfolk Solar FAQs

Do solar panels work well in Norfolk's climate?

Yes. Panels generate from daylight, not just direct sun. Norfolk averages ~4.0 peak sun hours and benefits from less urban pollution, providing excellent solar performance.

How much do solar panels cost in Norfolk?

Typical residential systems range £4,000—£8,000 depending on size and spec. With 0% VAT until 2027 and ~£1,950/yr savings, payback is usually 6—8 years.

Do I need planning permission in Norfolk?

Most homes are Permitted Development. Listed buildings, conservation areas, or flats may need Norfolk County Council approval — sq1i handles all checks and paperwork.

Do you cover rural Norfolk properties?

Yes — across all Norfolk including Norwich, King's Lynn, Great Yarmouth, Thetford, and rural villages. Agricultural buildings welcome.
