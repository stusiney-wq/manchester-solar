Manchester Solar FAQs

Do solar panels work well in Manchester’s climate?

Yes. Panels generate from daylight, not just direct sun. Manchester averages ~3.8 peak sun hours and modern panels work in diffuse light.

How much do solar panels cost in Manchester?

Typical residential systems range £4,000–£8,000 depending on size and spec. With 0% VAT until 2027 and ~£1,890/yr savings, payback is usually 6–8 years.

Do I need planning permission?

Most homes are Permitted Development. Listed buildings, conservation areas, or flats may need approval — checks are handled for you.

Do you cover business installs?

Yes — across Greater Manchester. We handle warehouses, schools, offices, retail and more.
